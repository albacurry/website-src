Posted by Alba Curry …

I am a PhD candidate at the University of California, Riverside, Department of Comparative Literature.

My research is in early Chinese philosophy, ancient Greek philosophy, feminist theory, philosophy of emotion, moral psychology, meta-ethics, and the ethics of Emotion A.I.

I also co-host a podcast called Let's Chat Ethics.
